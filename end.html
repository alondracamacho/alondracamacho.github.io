Is it really worth the watch?

I've only finished watching the series recently, roughly 2 months ago.

The show is notoriously known for its "bad ending".

Makes the fanbase wonder if they should just wait for the last couple of books to come out...NOT the best idea considering the last published book was released in 2011, and it is anticipated that there are 2 books left.

My take:

It won't feel like a waste of your time, even though you will most likely be underwhelmed by the ending.

The acting will get you to hold strong hatred towards certain characters, which will keep you captivated.

Watch at your own discretion.
