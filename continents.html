Time Frame:

Based off historical events, it's estimated to take place during 298 A.C. at the start of the show. Events shown are covering the span of 6 to 7 years.

Where?:

The series only truly covers two continents in the GOT world, Westeros and Essos. While Essos is the largest of the known continents, the show discloses a lot more information about Westeros.

Here are a few differences between the two:

Westeros

  • Plot is heavily focused here
  • Location of the Iron Throne
  • stonger military base
  • all ruled over one power - monarchy

Essos

  • more politically diverse
  • variety of cultures, languages, customs
  • complete map not available since less is known
