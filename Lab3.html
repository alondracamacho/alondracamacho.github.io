My Thoughts

It's safe to say that the prequel trilogy has an obvious unfair advantage to episodes 4 - 6, the higher production quality. The newer trilogy seemed to have leeched onto any originality from the first 2 trilogies and lacked any excitement that the first 6 films had(Dont even get me started on The Rise of Skywalker.
